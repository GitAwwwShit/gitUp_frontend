Git Goals: Set goals for your child to be more active and responsible.

Choose from a wide range of activities for you child. Use GitUp for any activity, even chores or homework.

Add goals for a sport in which your child may already participate. Set goals for the amount of time you want your child to play outside. Incentivize chores and homework time.

Git Progress: Track progress for each goal.

Input the amount of time your child spends on specific activities. Immediately see progress toward goal achievement. Show your child their personal dashboard to encourage future behaviors.

Git Rewards: Reward your child for achieving goals.

Create rewards for achieving goals. An example of a parent defined reward may be time on technology (TV or iPad). GitUp also offers badges which will display on your child's dashboard for application defined goals.
